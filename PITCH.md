## Your reading list, done — and ready to answer you.

**For master's & LLM students who have money but no time.**

You didn't enrol to spend weekends hunting PDFs across five library databases.
Send us your course outline. We find every reading, load it, and hand you a
private research assistant that **answers questions across your whole syllabus —
with the exact page it came from.**

### What you get
- **We source it for you.** Give us your syllabus; we build your course — statutes,
  cases, readings, all of it. You just log in.
- **Answers you can trust.** Every answer is cited to the document and **page**,
  with the exact passage on hover. No made-up cases. Ever.
- **Written like a distinction.** Argument-first, both sides weighed, authorities
  attributed — in plain, examiner-ready prose.
- **OSCOLA on tap.** One click turns any answer's sources into correct footnotes
  and a bibliography.
- **Exam Coach.** Paste (or upload) a past paper → it maps the facts, breaks it
  into issues, gathers the law for each, and assembles one referenced answer you
  can export to Word.
- **Weekly Update.** Upload your course outline → it lists every week's topic and
  writes an exam-standard summary of any week — exactly what you should have known
  by the end of it, grounded in your readings.
- **Deepen.** Turn a good answer into a top-band one: it steel-mans your thesis,
  takes one comparator into real depth, and resolves the trade-offs instead of
  listing them.
- **Essay, memo or report** format — your choice.

### Why not just NotebookLM / ChatGPT?
They hand you an empty box and hope you fill it. We hand you the **filled** box —
your exact readings, sourced and loaded — and every claim is traceable to the
page. That's the difference between a chatbot and a research assistant.
